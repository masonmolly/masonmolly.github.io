Transmission

A space invaders-eqsue arcade game, made using p5.js, for Global Game Jam 2018 (theme: Transmission). Play as a mosquito, attempting to infect (hit) all the humans within 30 seconds.

Balloon pop

Another twist on space invaders, made using p5.js. Move and shoot stones from the slingshot to pop all of the balloons in the time limit.

Snake

The classic arcade game, snake, made using p5.js. Move the snake using the arrow keys to collect food.

Magic Tourist

A short group VR project made for the Oculus Rift, using Unity. My role was to create an immersive environment, with the premise of the game being to explore and take pictures in an eerie, magical forest (with the ability to view your photos in a gallery).

View here (missing camera features & binaural audio)




inSight

A very simple demo I created from our group design for our Interaction Design product, inSight. Our product was an interactive noticeboard, aiming to faciliate communication between students in halls. The demo displays the use of colour interaction - the background gradient changes in response to how students in the flat report how they feel (through use of the face buttons).
