inSight

MADE WITH:     |   PLAY HERE   |   BROCHURE

inSight is a low-fidelity demo made by me for a group project in my Interaction Design module. inSight is designed to be an interactive noticeboard for students, in order to aid communication between flatmates.

This simple demo took a few hours to put together in p5.js (with all of the existing designs drawn up), made to present the concept of our product.

The prototype displays inSight's settings and note-editing menus, as well as a basic version of its mood-expressing function - press the button corresponding to your current mood, triggering a gradient background change to suggest the overall mood of the household.
