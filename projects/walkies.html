Walkies

MADE WITH:         |   THESIS   |   VIEW GAMEPLAY   |   DOWNLOAD

Walkies is an infinite runner-style game, made for my final year degree project. It is made in Unity, using C#, with all modelling and animation also made by myself in Blender.

The time frame for this project was around 2 months, accounting for learning time as this was an introductory project for me - in terms of getting to grips with Unity, and learning Blender from scratch.

A general aim for this project was to create a fun, infinite runner-style game, with cartoon-ish graphics, aiming to be accessible to a wide audience.

Walkies features a hub world, with suburban, town, and park areas (filled with basic AI) - each containing entrances to 4 infinite runner levels with varying difficulties and randomly generated obstacles and power-ups.
