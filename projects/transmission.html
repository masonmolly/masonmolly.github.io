Transmission

MADE WITH:       |   PLAY HERE   |   GGJ 2018   |   DOWNLOAD

Transmission is a space invaders-style game, made for Global Game Jam 2018. The theme for this game jam was transmission, hence the project theme of infection (transmitting disease as a mosquito).

Being a game jam, I developed Transmission in a time frame of around 48 hours. I used p5.js for the project, and GIMP for all of the pixel graphics.
