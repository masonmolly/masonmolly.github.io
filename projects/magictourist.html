Magic Tourist

MADE WITH:   |   VIEW GAMEPLAY (missing gameplay/audio features)

Magic Tourist is a walking photography simulator, developed for use with Oculus VR in Unity, for group project work in my 3D Environments and Modelling module.

Set within an eerie forest, the player can take pictures throughout the environment using Oculus touch controllers.

I was primarily responsible for creating an immersive environment, and thus utilised various particle effects, textures, terrain, lighting, and models.

The time frame for this project was around a month, with this being the first piece of work that introduced me to Unity.
