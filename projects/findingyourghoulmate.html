Finding Your Ghoulmate

MADE WITH:         |   VIEW GAMEPLAY   |   DOWNLOAD

Finding Your Ghoulmate is a cute, fast-paced game in which you are tasked with wading through ghouls to your boo each level, faced with a time limit. Features hiscores.

From conception, Ghoulmate took around a week to create with Blender, C#, and Unity. I am still currently improving the game, working on increasing the difficulty in later levels.
